# 💡 WALL-E Inspired Children's Desk Lamp
**Leaving Certificate Design & Communication Graphics Portfolio — 89%**

Designed a children's themed desk lamp modelled on the Pixar character WALL-E, produced as part of the Irish Leaving Certificate DCG course. The project followed the full engineering design process, from initial research and competitor analysis through to final CAD modelling and technical drawing production.

---

## 📐 Design Process
- **Design Research** — investigated desk lamp ergonomics, geometry, materials, power sources, and light sources
- **Competitor Analysis** — compared the Luminex Lamp and HÄRTE LED Work Lamp across shape, materials, power source, and light output
- **Concept Development** — explored multiple themes (rocket ship, dinosaur, WALL-E) before selecting WALL-E for its alignment with sustainability goals and child appeal
- **Freehand Sketching** — annotated sketches of components and full assembly
- **SolidWorks CAD** — full multi-part assembly with orthographic projections, exploded views, and photorealistic renders

---

## ⚙️ Key Design Features
| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Power | Foldable solar panel (USB-C connected) + lithium-ion battery backup |
| Light source | Smart LED strip with dimmer scroll control |
| Adjustability | Articulated neck to direct light |
| Eye comfort | Light diffuser panel to reduce glare and eye strain |
| Safety | Wide stable base, no sharp edges, child-safe materials (ABS plastic) |
| Theme | WALL-E character shape — yellow/dark colour scheme |

---
